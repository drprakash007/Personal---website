About Me

I am Dr. Prakasha S N, an expert in the field of Library and Information Science. With a passion for information management and dissemination, I am dedicated to advancing the field through research, teaching, and innovative practices. Prakasha S N, He has obtained his MSc in Library and Information Science from Bangalore University since 2015. In addition, He has obtained various Diploma & degree certificate in Library Science like Diploma in Information Sources and Library Services from IGNOU, Post-Graduate Diploma in Library Automation and Digitization, Post Graduate Diploma in Emerging Trends & Technologies in Library & Information Services from (IIT Delhi) through Swayam, and also has done Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Application (PGDCA). He received the “Student of the Year award” in Undergraduate, and also he secured “5th University Rank” holder in Library and Information Science at Bangalore University. He received “Innovation and Knowledge Excellence Global Young Researcher” Award from Shri. Paramahamsa Education & Research foundation Trust, New Delhi. He was cleared in UGC JRF, NET and SLET. Mr. Prakasha has been Member of MALA (Madaras Library Association) and He was served many institutions like NAL (National Aerospace laboratory), Bangalore, Dharmaram Vidya Kesthram, Bangalore and he was joined to Christ University as a Library Assistant. His also active researcher in Library and Information Science Discipline, he was participated and presented more than 20 National as well as International Conference, workshop and Seminar in various states of India. He published 15 Research and Conference Papers in UGC reputed Pre-Reviewed journals and Conference proceedings. Present his Full time (UGC-Senior Research Fellow) Ph.D. Scholar in Dept. of Library and Information Science, Bangalore University, Bangalore.
